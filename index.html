The History of Our Program

After-Life-Program (ALP) was established just four months ago. Additionally, ALP has hosted a range of workshops and events to help individuals connect with one another, share experiences, and find solace in a supportive environment. Despite being a young organization, ALP has already made a significant impact in the community and is committed to continuing to support those who are dealing with the mission of providing support and resources to individuals who are having trouble navigating the difficult journey of the third demension.
